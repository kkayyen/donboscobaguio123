PRE-REGISTRATION FOR ADMISSION SY: 2021-2022
Dear Parents and Guardians,
Greetings of Peace and Goodwill!
Don Bosco School of Baguio City, Inc.- Junior High School Department has now opened the online pre-registration for incoming grade 7 students and transferees for the school year 2021-2022. You can ensure a slot in our school by filling out all the necessary information required in this form.

Pursuant to Data Privacy Act 10173, by submitting this form, you are giving our school the permission to gather, utilize and store all gathered personal information filled out in this form to be used for admission purposes only. Rest assured that the data will be dealt with utmost confidentiality. Please be informed however, that filling out the pre-registration form would only ensure your child/ward's slot and would not necessarily mean that he/ she is officially enrolled. Necessary hard copy of the requirements for the admission must still be handed personally at the school during the scheduled enrollment dates to be announced via our official facebook page: DON BOSCO SCHOOL OF BAGUIO CITY, INC- jhs dept.
