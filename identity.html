1) The School Uniform. The school uniform is worn for a purpose. It symbolizes membership to a certain social group. The uniform of students identifies those who are enrolled in this school. It is therefore expected that the uniforms are wom properly at all times.

  1. Boys:
    • Grades 7-8: white short sleeves polo with Don Bosco logo
